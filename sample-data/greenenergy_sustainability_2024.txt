GREENENERGY SOLUTIONS
2024 Sustainability & Impact Report

ABOUT GREENENERGY SOLUTIONS

GreenEnergy Solutions is a leading renewable energy provider operating across Europe, with a focus on wind, solar, and energy storage solutions. We power over 2 million homes with 100% renewable energy and are committed to accelerating the global energy transition.

OUR MISSION
To deliver clean, affordable, and reliable renewable energy while creating positive social and environmental impact in the communities we serve.

CHALLENGES & CONTINUOUS IMPROVEMENT

1. Gender Representation: While we lead the industry at 40% women in workforce, we aim for 50% by 2030. Focused recruitment and retention strategies in place.

2. Scope 3 Emissions: Supply chain emissions remain a challenge. We are working with suppliers to set science-based targets and transition to low-carbon materials.

3. Community Acceptance: Some wind projects face local opposition. Enhanced early engagement and benefit-sharing models being implemented.

4. Grid Integration: Intermittency challenges require continued investment in storage and grid flexibility solutions.
