CHALLENGES & IMPROVEMENT AREAS

1. Safety Performance: While improving, our LTIFR of 0.48 is above our zero-harm aspiration. Enhanced focus on high-risk activities and behavioral safety.

2. Board Diversity: Women representation on board (22%) and independence (44%) below best practice. Active recruitment of qualified female and independent directors.

3. Renewable Energy: At 28%, we are behind our 50% target. Accelerating solar installations and exploring power purchase agreements.

4. Supply Chain Transparency: Limited Tier 2 visibility (45%) creates risks. Investing in supply chain mapping and engagement.

5. Gender Diversity: Women in management (18%) progressing slowly. Enhanced recruitment, mentorship, and flexible work policies.
